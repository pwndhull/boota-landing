Getting started

Up and running in a day.

1

Sign the partner agreement

Our partnership team will send you a simple one-page agreement. Covers pricing (8% per order), liability, and branding guidelines. No exclusivity, no lock-in.

2

Load your products

Import via CSV or use the admin dashboard to add products manually. We'll help you set up categories, images, and pricing. Most stores are ready within a few hours.

3

Set up your bays & slots

Tell us how many collection bays you have and what hours you want to offer. We'll configure your time slots (hourly recommended) and bay labels.

4

Download the app and go live

Install the Boota Store app on your iPad. Your first customer order can arrive within hours of going live on the platform.
