Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026  ·  Boota Pty Ltd ABN 00 000 000 000

Boota Pty Ltd (Boota, we, us, our) is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, hold, and disclose your personal information when you use our mobile application, website, and services (collectively, the Services).

By using the Services you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. What information we collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Identity information: Your name and mobile phone number (used for OTP authentication — we do not collect passwords).
  • Contact information: Delivery and collection address(es) in Australia.
  • Transaction information: Orders placed, items purchased, payment method type (card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Afterpay). We do not store full card numbers — these are handled by Stripe.
  • Vehicle information: Vehicle make, colour, and licence plate number, which you provide to help store staff identify your car at the collection bay.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version. We do not collect advertising identifiers.
  • Usage information: Features used, screens viewed, time spent in app, search queries within the app. This is used to improve the Services.
  • Location information: If you grant location permission, we use your approximate location to show nearby stores. We do not track your location continuously or in the background.

We do not collect sensitive information as defined by the Privacy Act (such as health information, racial or ethnic origin, or biometric data).

2. How we collect your information

We collect personal information directly from you when you:

  • Create an account or verify your phone number
  • Place an order or save an address
  • Contact our support team
  • Use the Boota app or website

We also collect information automatically through our app analytics and server logs when you use the Services.

3. How we use your information

We use your personal information to:

  • Create and manage your Boota account
  • Process and fulfil your orders
  • Send you order status notifications (push notifications and SMS)
  • Process payments via Stripe
  • Improve and personalise the Services
  • Detect and prevent fraud or security incidents
  • Comply with our legal obligations
  • Send you product updates and promotional offers (only with your consent; you may opt out at any time)

We will not use your personal information for any purpose that is incompatible with the purposes described above without your consent.

4. Disclosure of your information

We may share your personal information with:

  • Partner stores: We share your name, order details, vehicle information, and collection slot with the store fulfilling your order. Stores are contractually required to handle this information in accordance with applicable privacy laws and only for the purpose of fulfilling your order.
  • Stripe: Our payment processor. Stripe processes card payments and is PCI DSS compliant. See stripe.com/au/privacy.
  • Supabase: Our database and authentication provider. Data is stored on servers located in Sydney, Australia (AWS ap-southeast-2 region).
  • Law enforcement or government agencies: Where required by law, court order, or to protect Boota's rights.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information for advertising purposes.

5. Data storage and security

Your personal information is stored on servers located in Sydney, Australia. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Row-level security policies ensuring each user can only access their own data
  • OTP-based authentication (no passwords stored)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Section 8 below). We may retain transaction records for up to 7 years to comply with Australian taxation law.

7. Cookies and tracking

Our website uses essential cookies required for its operation (session management). We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. Our mobile app does not use cookies.

8. Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or out-of-date personal information
  • Delete your account and personal information (subject to our legal obligations)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Complain about a privacy breach

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@boota.com.au. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

9. Children's privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify you via the app or email if the changes are material. Your continued use of the Services after the changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact our Privacy Officer:

  • Email: privacy@boota.com.au
  • Post: Privacy Officer, Boota Pty Ltd, Sydney NSW Australia
