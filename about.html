Our mission

Give Australians back the time they spend on groceries.

The average Australian spends 4.5 hours a week on grocery shopping — including travel, parking, shopping, and checkout. That's 234 hours a year. Nearly 10 full days.

We think that time could be better spent. With Boota, your shop takes 10 minutes online, and collection takes 3 minutes at the store. The rest of your day is yours.

We also believe the local independent grocer shouldn't lose to the big chains just because they can't afford a click-and-collect platform. Boota levels that playing field.

What we believe

Our values.

⚡

Speed is kindness

Every second we save a customer is a second they get back. We obsess over latency, load times, and how quickly staff can pack an order.

🔐

Security, not security theatre

OTP-only auth, server-side payments, row-level security on every table. You can't pay your way to a loophole here.

🏪

Local first

We serve independent Australian grocers. No multinationals. Every partner store is locally owned and community-focused.

🌱

Less waste

Planned orders mean less impulse buying and less food waste. A better shop for you is a better outcome for the planet.

📡

Real-time, always

If something changes — an item is out of stock, your order is ready, your bay is assigned — you know instantly. No polling, no refreshing.

🇦🇺

Australian through and through

Sydney servers, AUD pricing, GST-inclusive, AU phone numbers, Afterpay support. We're not a US startup that added a dollar sign.

Our journey

From frustration to launch.

AUG 2024
The idea
Co-founders Pawan and Jamie sketch the first version of Boota on a napkin at a café in Surry Hills, after a particularly bad Woolworths car park experience.
NOV 2024
First store partner
FreshMart Surry Hills becomes Boota's first store partner. Their first week on the platform generates 47 orders — more than their entire previous month of phone pre-orders.
FEB 2025
App Store launch
Boota launches on iOS and Android in Sydney. 500 downloads in the first week. 4.8-star rating on day one.
JUN 2025
Melbourne expansion
Boota expands to Melbourne with 12 new store partners in Fitzroy, Collingwood, and South Yarra.
2026
50,000 orders. Growing.
40+ stores, 50k+ completed orders, 4.9-star rating, and expanding to Brisbane later this year.
