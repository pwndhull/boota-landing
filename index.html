How it works

Three steps.
Zero stress.

From browse to boot in less time than it takes to find a park.

1
🛍️

Browse & add to cart

Shop from your local Boota store. Fresh produce, dairy, meat, pantry — all at in-store prices. No markup, no subscription.

2
🕐

Pick a time slot

Choose when you want to collect — hourly slots from 8am to 8pm, 7 days a week. Pay securely via card, Apple Pay, Google Pay or Afterpay.

3
🚗

Drive in, stay in your car

Tap "I'm Here" when you arrive. Staff see your bay assignment instantly and bring your packed bags straight to your boot. Done.

Features

Built for real life
in Australia.

⚡

Real-time order tracking

Watch your order move from Confirmed → Packing → Ready live. No refreshing — the app updates itself the moment anything changes.

🅿️

Dedicated boot bays

Drive into your assigned bay. Staff meet you in under 3 minutes. No circling the car park, no parking anxiety.

🔄

Substitutions, your way

If something's out of stock, you'll get a push notification with a suggested substitute. Accept or skip — you're always in control.

💳

All AU payment methods

Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Afterpay. Prices include GST — no surprises at checkout.

🔔

Smart notifications

We notify you when packing starts, when your order is ready, and when your bay is assigned — nothing more, nothing less.

🔐

Privacy first

Phone OTP login — no passwords to forget. Your data lives in Australia (Sydney servers). We never sell your information.

Live tracking

Your order, updating in real time.

The moment staff start packing your order, your app knows about it. When your bay is assigned, you get a notification — not a refresh button.
